Dr. Ezra founded Empower Fitness after years of witnessing the gap in traditional physical therapy — patients discharged at "pain-free" and left to figure out the rest on their own. He built a different model: one that takes you from injury all the way through to full function, performance, and long-term resilience.

Today, Dr. Ezra serves as Founder and Clinical Director, designing and overseeing every program Empower Fitness delivers. While our growing team of licensed physical therapists and certified trainers works with clients directly, Dr. Ezra's methodology, standards, and clinical precision are behind every session. When you work with Empower Fitness, you're getting a DPT-caliber program — not just a workout.
