FAQ: PT in Deerfield Beach

Common questions from Deerfield Beach residents.

Do you serve Century Village and other 55+ communities in Deerfield Beach?
Yes. Empower Fitness regularly serves residents in Century Village and other Deerfield Beach active adult communities. Dr. Ezra is experienced working in condo and community settings.
Can you help with fall prevention in older adults?
Fall prevention is one of Dr. Ezra's core specialties. He conducts a thorough balance and strength assessment, then builds a progressive program that dramatically reduces fall risk — all delivered in your own home environment.
What if I need PT after a hip or knee replacement?
Post-surgical rehab is ideal for in-home care. Dr. Ezra will coordinate with your surgeon's protocol and provide hands-on therapy, gait training, and progressive strengthening — all without you having to get into a car in the early recovery days.
Is concierge PT affordable for Deerfield Beach retirees?
Most Empower Fitness clients find that 60-minute 1-on-1 sessions (even out-of-pocket) cost the same or less than the true cost of clinic PT once you factor in co-pays, multiple visits, and transportation. HSA/FSA can be used.
