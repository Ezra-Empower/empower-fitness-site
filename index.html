Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Have a question not answered here? Call us or book a free consultation — we'll answer everything.

Do I need a doctor's referral to start?

No referral needed. Florida is a direct access state — you can start physical therapy with Dr. Ezra immediately, without a physician referral. Just book your free consultation and we'll handle the rest.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Boca Raton, Delray Beach, Pompano Beach, Deerfield Beach, Boynton Beach, Highland Beach, and surrounding South Florida communities. If you're unsure, reach out — we'll let you know if we can come to you.

Do you accept insurance?

Empower Fitness is a private-pay, concierge practice. We do not bill insurance directly. However, we can provide a superbill for you to submit for potential out-of-network reimbursement. Many clients find the one-on-one quality is well worth the investment.

How much does concierge PT cost in Boca Raton?

Rates vary by service and plan. Physical therapy in Boca Raton typically ranges from $100–$600 per session. At Empower Fitness, you're paying for a DPT who comes to you, works with only you for the entire session, and builds a plan specifically for your goals. Schedule a free consultation for exact pricing.

What does physical therapy actually do?

PT helps you recover from injuries, manage chronic pain, restore mobility, and prevent future injuries. Dr. Ezra uses targeted exercises, manual therapy, and movement retraining — all tailored to your specific condition and goals.

Is physical therapy really effective?

Yes — and the research is clear. PT addresses root causes of pain, not just symptoms. For most conditions — from post-surgical recovery to chronic back pain — PT produces better long-term outcomes than medication or rest alone.

How often should I do physical therapy?

Frequency depends on your condition and goals. Acute recovery may require 2–3 sessions per week initially, while maintenance or longevity programs are often 1–2 times per week. Dr. Ezra will recommend a schedule that fits your life and accelerates your progress.

What should I expect in my first session?

Your first session is a comprehensive evaluation — Dr. Ezra will assess your movement, discuss your history and goals, and begin building your personalized plan. Many clients leave feeling better after session one. It's thorough, not rushed.
