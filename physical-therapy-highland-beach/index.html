FAQ: PT in Highland Beach

Common questions from Highland Beach residents.

Is Empower Fitness fully private and discreet?
Yes. All sessions are 1-on-1 in your private home — no other patients, no shared equipment, no front desk. Complete discretion is a core part of the concierge model.
How far is Dr. Ezra willing to travel to Highland Beach?
Highland Beach is within Empower Fitness's primary service area (it's approximately 5 miles from the main Boca Raton base). Travel is never an issue for Highland Beach visits.
Can you provide a higher-frequency program for accelerated recovery?
Absolutely. For clients who want to accelerate recovery or achieve specific performance goals, Dr. Ezra can schedule multiple weekly sessions and build a comprehensive home program with check-ins between visits.
Do you offer standalone longevity fitness training (not just injury rehab)?
Yes. Many Highland Beach clients work with Dr. Ezra not for injury rehab, but for proactive longevity programming — building strength, balance, mobility, and cardiovascular fitness to stay active into their 70s, 80s, and beyond.
