Pain-Free Isn't the Same as Ready to Compete

Most physical therapy programs stop when your pain stops. At Empower Fitness, that's where we're just getting started. Return-to-sport PT bridges the gap between injury recovery and full athletic performance — rebuilding sport-specific strength, movement quality, and confidence so you come back better than before.

Dr. Ezra Miller, PT, DPT delivers one-on-one, in-home physical therapy across South Florida. No waiting rooms, no rushed 15-minute sessions, no generic protocols. Just expert sport-specific care at your home, gym, or facility.
