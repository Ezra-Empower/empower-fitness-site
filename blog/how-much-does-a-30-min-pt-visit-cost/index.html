You should see a PT 1 to 3 times per week, depending on your condition and treatment goals. Your provider will recommend the right frequency based on your progress.

How Much Is a Physical Therapy Session with Insurance?

A physical therapy session with insurance can cost from $100 to $600 per visit, depending on your plan and provider network. Some plans may cover a set number of sessions fully, while others apply a deductible or require prior authorization.

How Do I Know If My PT Is Covered by Insurance?

You can know if your PT is covered by insurance by contacting your provider directly. Ask about coverage details, referral requirements, session limits, and any applicable deductibles.

Is It Worth Paying for a PT?

Yes, it is worth paying for a PT. Professional physical therapy can help you recover faster, reduce pain, avoid surgery, and improve long-term mobility and function. Investing in expert care often leads to better outcomes and a quicker return to your daily activities or sport.
