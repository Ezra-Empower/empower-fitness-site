Pricing FAQ

Common questions about our pricing model.

Why doesn't Empower Fitness accept insurance?
Insurance contracts dictate session lengths, visit limits, billing codes, and documentation requirements — not your clinical needs. By operating outside of insurance, Dr. Ezra can give you 60 uninterrupted minutes per session, see you as long as needed to achieve your goals, and make clinical decisions based on what's best for you — not what's reimbursable. Most clients find the true cost comparable once they factor in co-pays, deductibles, and lost time.
Can I get reimbursed by my insurance for Empower Fitness sessions?
Potentially, yes. Empower Fitness provides detailed superbills (itemized receipts with CPT codes) that you can submit directly to your insurance for out-of-network reimbursement consideration. Reimbursement amounts vary widely by plan — some clients recover 40–70% of costs, others receive nothing. Call your insurance to ask about out-of-network PT benefits before your first session.
Do package sessions expire?
Recovery Packages (8 sessions) are valid for 12 weeks from the date of purchase — plenty of time to complete a full course of treatment. Longevity Memberships are ongoing month-to-month with unused sessions carrying over for 30 days.
Is there a cancellation fee?
We ask for 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations (within 24 hours) may be charged 50% of the session rate. We understand life happens and apply this policy with common sense.
What if I'm not sure which plan is right for me?
Start with the free 15-minute consultation. After hearing about your situation, Dr. Ezra will recommend the plan that matches your goals and budget — with zero pressure to upsize. Many clients start with a single session to experience the care before committing to a package.
