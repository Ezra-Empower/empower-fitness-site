1

Assessment & Goals

Functional Return

Return to daily activities (stairs, lifting, bending) without pain or compensation. Full function restoration is our standard, not the exception.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need a doctor's referral for in-home PT?
No. Florida law allows direct access to physical therapy. You can schedule with us without a referral. However, if you have insurance, we can coordinate with your doctor for documentation and billing purposes.
Q: How long are in-home PT sessions?
Standard sessions are 60 minutes, which includes evaluation, hands-on therapy, exercise instruction, and home program refinement. Initial assessments may run 75 minutes.
Q: How often should I schedule in-home PT?
Frequency depends on your condition and goals. Most acute conditions benefit from 2–3 sessions per week for 4–6 weeks, then transition to 1–2 sessions weekly. We adjust based on your progress.
Q: Does insurance cover in-home physical therapy?
Many insurance plans cover PT services regardless of location (clinic vs. home). We verify your coverage during your free consultation and discuss out-of-pocket options if needed.
Q: What areas do you service for in-home PT?
We provide in-home PT throughout South Florida, including Boca Raton, Delray Beach, Pompano Beach, and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm service availability for your address.
