Quick Answers

Questions most people ask first.

Do you take insurance?

No — and that's a good thing. Insurance drives the 20-minute session model. Cash-pay means we answer to you, not a billing code. Most clients find the real cost is lower once they stop paying repeatedly for a problem that never gets solved.

What does a session look like?

We come to you. The full hour is hands-on — manual therapy, movement work, strength — based on what you need that day. No aides, no machines, no watching a timer until you're dismissed.

How is this different from a clinic?

At a clinic: different therapist each visit, 15–20 minutes of real care, discharged when the pain eases. With us: same doctor every time, full hour, and we don't stop until you're fully functional — not just out of pain.

How do I know if this is right for me?

That's what the consult is for. Tell us what's going on and we'll give you an honest answer about whether we can help and what it would look like. No pressure, no pitch.

Where do you work?

Your home, your club, your gym, your workplace — wherever works for you. If you're in Boca Raton, Delray Beach, or surrounding South Florida, we come to you.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients feel a meaningful difference within 2–4 sessions. That's the honest answer based on years of doing this — not a guarantee, but a realistic expectation for most people we work with.
