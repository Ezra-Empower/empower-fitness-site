Why Nutrition Is Half the Battle

Physical therapy and fitness training build the foundation. Nutrition builds the results. Your body requires specific nutrients to heal injuries, adapt to training, lose fat, build muscle, and maintain energy. Without aligned nutrition, you're leaving gains on the table.

The synergy works both ways: Recovery from PT requires protein and micronutrients for tissue healing. Training adaptation requires proper carbs and protein timing. Weight loss requires caloric awareness. Performance requires fueling.

✓ PT + Nutrition = Faster Healing

Proper nutrient timing and macronutrient balance accelerate tissue repair, reduce inflammation, and restore function faster than PT alone. Your nutrition supports your therapy.

✓ Training + Nutrition = Visible Results

Strength training without proper nutrition leaves you plateaued. Add aligned nutrition and you see measurable strength gains, fat loss, and muscle development within weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I have to follow a specific diet (keto, vegan, etc.)?
No. We work within your preferences and lifestyle. If you're vegan, we build a vegan plan with proper nutrition. If you prefer Mediterranean style, that works too. The framework is flexible—your preferences are respected.
Q: How often do I meet with the nutrition coach?
Initial nutrition consultation is included with most services. Follow-up check-ins are typically monthly, but can be adjusted based on your needs and goals. Between sessions, the food tracking app provides ongoing feedback.
Q: What if I have food allergies or restrictions?
We absolutely account for allergies, intolerances, and restrictions. Whether it's gluten-free, dairy-free, nut-free, or any other constraint, we design a plan that works within those limitations while meeting your nutritional goals.
Q: Can nutrition guidance help with my PT recovery?
Absolutely. Proper nutrition accelerates tissue healing after injury or surgery. We coordinate nutrition recommendations with your PT plan to maximize recovery speed and outcomes. Protein timing, micronutrient status, and anti-inflammatory foods all matter.
Q: Do I need to buy expensive supplements?
No. We recommend cost-effective, evidence-based supplements. Many clients get 80% of the benefit from basic protein powder, multivitamin, and joint health supplements. We focus on value and necessity, not upselling.
