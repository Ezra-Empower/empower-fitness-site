Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What equipment do I need at my training location?
We work with what you have. Your trainer will assess your space and use minimal equipment: dumbbells, resistance bands, a bench, and your bodyweight. We can also recommend affordable equipment purchases if you want to optimize your setup.
Q: How often should I train for best results?
Most clients benefit from 2-3 training sessions per week combined with proper nutrition and recovery. Your trainer will recommend a frequency based on your goals, current fitness level, and schedule.
Q: Can I train if I'm recovering from an injury?
Yes. Dr. Ezra specializes in programming for post-injury clients. We modify exercises to work around your limitations and progressively rebuild strength safely. However, if you need active rehab, our physical therapy services may be the better starting point.
Q: Do you offer nutrition guidance alongside training?
Yes! We offer nutrition and supplement guidance as an add-on service. Optimal results require training + proper nutrition. Your trainer will coordinate with our nutrition guidance to maximize outcomes.
Q: What if I travel or have a busy schedule?
Training adapts to your schedule—we come to you wherever that is. If you travel, we can build home/hotel workout routines you can do independently and resume supervised sessions when you return.
